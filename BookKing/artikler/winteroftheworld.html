Winter of the World

Author: Ken Follett

Published: 18 September 2012

Pages: 940

Summary

The novel follows the second generation of the families ,born to the main characters of the first novel, Fall of Giants and is set during World War 2 and some years after.

Personal Opinion

This novel must be read after Fall of Giants as the consequences of the characters actions in the first book becomes more apparent.

Personally liked the characters better in the first novel, but the characters here are still great.

Review by: André Eliassen
