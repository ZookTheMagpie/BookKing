Gylendals tables & formulas in Physics (Physics 1 & 2)

Author: John Haugan & Eimund Aamot

Published: 7 November 2014

Pages: 48

Summary

The book is a support to the work of the subjects, the tests and the examination. The brochures provide a brief overview of the key formulas and procedures in the courses. In addition, the table values of selected sizes and symbols. You will also find appropriate formulas to preparatory course for engineering.

Personal Opinion

This book is a golden nugget of knowledge and prised by students. It’s concentrated and easy to find knowledge makes this a must at all tests and exams.
