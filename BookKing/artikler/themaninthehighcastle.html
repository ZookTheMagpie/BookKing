The Man in the High Castle

Author: Philip.K.Dick

Published: January 1 1962

Pages: 239

Summary

The Man in the High Castle is set in 1962, fifteen years after an alternative ending to World War II, where the novel concerns intrigues between the victorious Axis Powers—Imperial Japan and Nazi Germany— as they rule over the former United States, as well as daily life under the resulting totalitarian rule.

Personal Opinion

An interesting read of how the world could have been both good and bad. The characters seem real, and the plot follows a logical path to how it ends.

Highly recommend it for anyone that has ever thought about what would have happened if the Axis won.

Review by: André Eliassen
