Prince of Thorns

Author: Mark Lawrence

Published: 2 August 2011

Pages: 384

Summary

From being a privileged royal child, raised by a loving mother, Jorg Ancrath has become the Prince of Thorns, a charming, immoral boy leading a grim band of outlaws in a series of raids and atrocities. The world is in chaos: violence is rife, nightmares everywhere. Jorg has the ability to master the living and the dead, but there is still one thing that puts a chill in him. Returning to his father's castle Jorg must confront horrors from his childhood and carve himself a future with all hands turned against him.

Personal Opinion

If you expect a casual happy read, this book is not for you. This book satisfies the dark part of you that wants to see what would happen if the evil characters were in focus.

Review by: Hallvard Bjørdalsbakke
