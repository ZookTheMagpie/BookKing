I am Legend

Author: Richard Matheson

Published: 1954

Pages: 160

Summary

Robert Neville is the apparent sole survivor of a pandemic whose symptoms resemble vampirism. It is said that the pandemic was caused by a war, and that it was spread by dust storms in the cities and an explosion in the mosquito population. The narrative details Neville's daily life in Los Angeles as he attempts to comprehend, research, and possibly cure the disease, to which he is immune.

Personal Opinion

An ok book it is a slow read at time despite being so short.

Review by: André Eliassen
